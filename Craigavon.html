Craigavon Path


10 mins     730m

About the path

Combine walking the dog, a play in the playground and enjoy a scenic walk through bush while at Craigavon Park. The path itself is mainly gravel and would suit a sturdy buggy. There is also a circuit of fitness stations along the way nestled into the bush. The dog park offers a range of obstacles and structures for you and your dog to play in. There is a simple kids playground alongside the car park which you can enjoy before or after your walk.
