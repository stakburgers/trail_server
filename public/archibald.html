10 mins     20 mins     1.5km



About the path

Archibald Park path is part of the Te Whau Pathway and offers a safe off-road shared path. This is great if you or your kids are learning to ride a bike, and want to combine a bike ride with a kick around at the sports fields or picnic. There are some well-positioned picnic tables with views over the Whau River. Easy parking and public toilets also are available off Beaubank Road.
