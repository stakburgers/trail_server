Ambury Farm Path


10 mins    700m

About the path

Bring the whānau to meet some farm animals on this fun walk around Ambury Farm in Māngere Bridge. The path is a mix of grass path and gravel tracks. There is pushchair and wheelchair access around the farm animal enclosures, though these may be inaccessible in wet conditions. From the car park, head toward the woolshed and milking shed. Wander through the grassy lanes between the animal enclosures and take your time getting to know the animals. You can see sheep, lambs (seasonal from mid July), goats, cows, pigs, chickens, turkey, rabbits and maybe an elusive peacock. You're welcome to enter the paddocks to mingle with the animals, except during lambing season (mid-July and early August). Please leave the gates as you find them.
