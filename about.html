About Us

Auckland Paths, often referred to as aklpath, is a valuable online resource for enthusiasts of biking and walking trails in the Auckland region of New Zealand. This user-friendly website offers interactive maps that vividly display trails, allowing users to explore and plan their outdoor adventures.Moreover, Auckland Paths provides essential information on accessing trailheads, nearby amenities, and even safety tips. With its wealth of resources, this platform has become an indispensable tool for both locals and visitors looking to discover the natural beauty and recreational opportunities that Auckland has to offer.
